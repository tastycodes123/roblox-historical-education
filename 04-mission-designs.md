## Mission 1: The Water Carrier's Trial

**Type:** Tutorial | **Time:** 8-12 minutes | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Brief:** Deliver water to three work gangs without spilling.

**Learning Goals:**
- Water scarcity in desert construction
- Pyramid layout and navigation
- Physical labor difficulty in heat

**Flow:**
1. Fill water skin at well
2. Navigate to Red Gang lower ramp (avoid sledge traffic)
3. Deliver to workers
4. Repeat for Blue Gang (mid-ramp, harder)
5. Repeat for Gold Gang (upper ramp, hardest)

**Rewards:** 50 knowledge points (Stone Movement), unlock Tool Assistant path

---

## Mission 2: The Broken Rope

**Type:** Problem-solving | **Time:** 12-15 minutes | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Brief:** Father's gang lost rope. Find replacement before evening or lose honor.

**Flow:**
1. Navigate to Supply Depot
2. Talk to Scribe (need authorization)
3. Get gang seal OR overseer signature (player choice)
4. Return with proof
5. Deliver rope to gang before sunset

**Rewards:** 75 knowledge points, gang reputation boost

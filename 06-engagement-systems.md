# Agent 6: Engagement Systems - Ancient Egypt Pyramid

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-18

---

## System 1: Historical Knowledge Tree ⭐ ESSENTIAL

**What It Is:** Progressive unlocking where players access new areas by learning construction techniques.

**Six Tiers:**
1. Water Carrier (Start) - Village access
2. Tool Assistant - Workshops, quarry observation
3. Apprentice Stone Cutter - Quarry floor, cutting stations
4. Skilled Worker - Mid-pyramid, positioning crews
5. Gang Leader - Upper pyramid, overseer meetings
6. Master Craftsman - Pyramid peak, architect platform

**Knowledge Domains:**
- Stone Movement
- Measurement & Alignment
- Tool Mastery
- Social Navigation
- Construction Techniques

**Monetization:** FREE all tiers (20-30hr), Premium 2× rate (10-15hr)

---

## System 2: Artifact Collection ⭐ ESSENTIAL

**What It Is:** 50 historical artifacts (reduced from 75) hidden throughout site.

**Categories:**
- Tools (15): Chisels, hammers, measuring instruments
- Inscriptions (10): Quarry marks, graffiti
- Personal (10): Amulets, games
- Food (8): Bread molds, grain
- Construction (7): Stone chips, rope
- Administrative (5 - RARE): Papyrus records

**Rarity:** Common 60% | Uncommon 30% | Rare 9% | Legendary 1%

**Discovery:** Contextual placement, visual sparkles, 3D examination with historical info

**Monetization:** FREE (all discoverable), Premium Detector (shows direction only)

---

## System 3: Work Gang Reputation ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY

**Four Gangs:**
- Red Gang: "Khufu's Strong" - Strength challenges
- Blue Gang: "Enduring" - Precision
- Gold Gang: "Swift" - Speed
- Green Gang: "Wise" - Problem-solving

**System:** Join gang, contribute points through missions, leaderboard, visual pyramid progress
